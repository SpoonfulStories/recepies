Spoonful Stories - Spaghetti Recipes

1. Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A hearty Italian dish with rich meat sauce made with beef, tomatoes, and fragrant herbs.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion, diced
  • Garlic, minced
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano, basil, salt, pepper
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, and basil. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve over cooked spaghetti and top with Parmesan cheese.

2. Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

This simple, classic recipe features garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes for a quick and flavorful meal.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic, thinly sliced
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic, and sauté until golden.
  3. Toss spaghetti in the garlic oil, season with red pepper flakes and parsley.
  4. Serve with Parmesan cheese for extra flavor.

3. Creamy Spaghetti Carbonara

This rich and indulgent dish combines crispy pancetta and a creamy egg-Parmesan sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta or bacon, diced
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti.
  2. Fry pancetta or bacon until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Toss hot spaghetti with pancetta, and stir in egg mixture quickly to form a creamy sauce.
  5. Season with black pepper and serve.

4. Spaghetti with Marinara Sauce

A simple and healthy dish with homemade marinara sauce made from ripe tomatoes, garlic, and fresh basil. Perfect for vegetarians.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomatoes (fresh or canned)
  • Onion, diced
  • Garlic, minced
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt, pepper, and oregano

Instructions:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add tomatoes and herbs, then simmer for 20-30 minutes until thickened.
  3. Toss the sauce with cooked spaghetti, and top with fresh basil and Parmesan.

5. Spaghetti Puttanesca

This bold and tangy recipe is made with tomatoes, olives, capers, garlic, and anchovies for a unique and flavorful experience.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic, minced
  • Anchovy fillets
  • Olives, pitted and halved
  • Capers
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti.
  2. Sauté garlic and anchovies until fragrant.
  3. Add olives, capers, and tomatoes. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Toss with spaghetti and serve with red pepper flakes for extra heat.
